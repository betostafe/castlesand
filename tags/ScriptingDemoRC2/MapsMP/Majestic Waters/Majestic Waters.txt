Among all the mountains and lakes, an underground network of flooded caverns is present in this area. Inside these caverns, a royal family hid their valuable treasures a long time ago. Greedy generals then killed the royals and tried to take the treasures for themselves. But as the story goes, the royal family still haunts the Majestic Waters, looking down on them from the skies. And he who enters the caverns, never returns...||NOTE: Location 7 is a spectator location who can view the entire map but is not able to play.||Made by The Dark Lord.
