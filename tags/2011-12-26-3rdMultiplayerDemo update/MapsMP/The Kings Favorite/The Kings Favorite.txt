Title
The King's Favorite

SmallDesc
A 1 vs 1 multiplayer map made by GreatWhiteBear.

BigDesc
A 1 vs 1 multiplayer map made by GreatWhiteBear. The king had two sons, Wychor and Matthyor. They were always trying to get the most attention of their father who got sick of it. They asked him to give them a piece of land to fight out on who was their fathers favorite.
