Title
Besieged

SmallDesc
A multiplayer map by Ben

BigDesc
A 4-Player Multiplayer mission by Ben similar to castle slaughter, but with a twist. Four castles stand in the middle of an open plain.
Train huge armies and destroy your enemy's castles, but protect your farms, lest famine take hold of your people!
Recommended teams are 1+3 and 2+4.
