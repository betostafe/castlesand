Implemented:
!BLOCK_TRADE <Ware ID> - Disallows trading of this resource for current player
!ADD_WARE_TO <HOUSE_TYPE_ID> <HOUSE_NUMBER> <WARE_ID> <QUANTITY> - Adds resources to any house
!SET_AI_CHARACTER EQUIP_RATE <TICKS> - Number of game ticks between AI training soldiers (default=1000)


Examples:
!BLOCK_TRADE 1 - Disallows trading of stone for the current player
!ADD_WARE_TO 27 1 8 5 - Adds 5 wine to the 1st Inn of the current player
!SET_AI_CHARACTER EQUIP_RATE 300 - AI of current player will train soldiers every 30 seconds
