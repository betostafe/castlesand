Title
Invasion

SmallDesc
Special fighting map by Skypper

BigDesc
Every 90 seconds there will come a new wave to destory your base. There are 60 waves to defeat with a total amount of 9.605 soldiers. For every wave you survive you get some weapons and recruits the amount of weapons and recruits will increase when you got further. Good luck.||Visit the forum www.knightsandmerchants.net/forum/ for tips to defeat all the 60 waves.
